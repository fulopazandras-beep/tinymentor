Privacy Policy

Tiny Mentor · Last updated: 17 June 2026

Tiny Mentor is a learning app for young children, made to be used together with a parent or guardian. We care about your family's privacy. This page explains how the app handles information.

The short version

Tiny Mentor stores everything on your own device and sends nothing to us or anyone else. There are no accounts, no servers, no ads, and no tracking.

What is stored (on your device only)

So the app can work, the following is saved in your device's local storage:

  • the child's first name (only what you type)
  • the chosen character and age range
  • game progress, stars and stickers
  • the settings you choose

This information never leaves your device. We cannot see it, and it is never sent to any server or third party.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require an account, sign-up or email.
  • We do not show advertisements.
  • We do not use analytics or third-party tracking.
  • We do not share or sell data — because we never receive any.

Children's privacy

Tiny Mentor is intended for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. Because the app does not collect or transmit personal information, it does not knowingly gather data from children. The parental gate and consent step let a grown-up set up and oversee use.

Your control

All data stays on your device. You can remove it at any time using “Reset progress” in the Parent area, or by clearing the app's data / uninstalling the app.

Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes how it handles information (for example, if a future feature needs the internet), we will update this page before that feature is turned on.
